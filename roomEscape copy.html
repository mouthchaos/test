FAQ

Q. Can we just show up?
A. Showing up without a booking is risky business. We can book weeks in advance and strongly recommend you make a booking to ensure you can play. We get especially busy on weekends and there is absolutely no guarantee that the room you want will be available if you do not have a booking.
Q. The internet says it might snow! Can I cancel my booking without penalty?
A. You can always cancel in accordance with our cancellation policy. Snow and other weather conditions are part of living in Canada. Escape Games Canada is an indoor facility and operates in all weather conditions. In the event of severe weather which results in major road closures, a rain check date for your mission may be offered. We may also offer a rain check in the event of a Government-issued state of emergency or a service outage that prohibits us from honouring your booking.
Q. I have a 14 year-old, can they play with us?
A. All players have to be 16 years old, or older, but we can make some exceptions. Depending on the mission you choose, and how many minors are in the group, we may be able to accommodate you. Please call us to find out!
Q. How many people can play?
A. The rooms are designed to be played with groups of 4 or more players. We recommend a group of 6 to 8 for ideal gameplay. Playing with fewer than the minimum number of players required means you’ll have to purchase the minimum number of tickets. It can also be challenging or damn near impossible to win.
Q. Can I play by myself?
A. No! For safety reasons we will not let you try the room on your own. It would be nearly impossible to solve some of the puzzles in the missions unless you have mastered the art of manipulating the space-time continuum or have multiple appendages like Vishnu. If you are Vishnu, you are more than welcome to try.
